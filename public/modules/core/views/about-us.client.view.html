About Us

Our Purpose – To help make purchase decision simpler for consumers in Africa.

Here are a few things about Raytee

  • Raytee was founded in 2016 to help people find great and amazing brands like budding start-ups, mechanics, wedding planners, financial services, and many more.
  • Raytee will also help you find notorious brands that consistently deliver bad service thereby saving time, money, and of course heartache.
  • All users (Rayteers) including businesses can sign up for free and integrate Raytee into any online platform for timely feedback and reviews from customers
  • With an estimated target of 1million reviews by Q1 2017, Raytee aims to deliver unparalleled support for brands and consumers
  • In Q2 2017, Raytee will launch it analytics division “DataWave” to assist brands and investors make informed business decisions in Nigeria and across Africa
  • Raytee makes money from selling ads to local businesses – this doesn’t affect or reorder their reviews or ratings in anyway.
  • Users can access Raytee via its most responsive website with its mobile apps for various platform coming on-stream in Q4 2016
  • We have a vision to integrate all local businesses on Raytee in various Africa countries into a review powerhouse by Q1 2018.

Be a part of this journey. Raytee We make it Simpler.
